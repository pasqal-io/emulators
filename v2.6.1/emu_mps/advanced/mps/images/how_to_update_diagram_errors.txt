flowchart TD
  Start([Start of time-step]) --> SweepLR["Sweep Left → Right"]
  SweepLR --> Pair{"For each pair (j, j+1)"}

  Pair --> BuildHeff["Build effective 2-site Hamiltonian H_ef(j, j+1)"]
  BuildHeff --> ErrorA["Error A: Approximate long-range interactions (partial trace over distant qubits)"]

  BuildHeff --> Evolve["Numerically evolve the pair under H_eff"]
  Evolve --> ErrorB["Error B: Numerical / Lanczos error (Krylov subspace, finite precision) [controled with precesion and extra_krylov_tolerance]"]

  Evolve --> Split["After evolution, split via SVD"]
  Split --> ErrorC["Error C: Truncation error (dropping small singular values) [controlled with max_bond_dim and precision]"]

  Split --> Reinsert["Re-insert tensors into MPS"]

  Reinsert --> SweepRL["Sweep Right → Left (repeat once but in reverse order)"]

  SweepRL -->Pair
  SweepRL --> End(["End of time-step"])

  End --> ErrorD["Error D: Looping / Trotter-like sweep error (because we do many local updates)"]
